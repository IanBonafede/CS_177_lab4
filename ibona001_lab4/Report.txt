---------design-----------

I changed most of the program. Each place you can visit has a facility that the shuttle busses uses to get in line and pick people up. There is an event set for boarding the shuttles(1 per shuttle). Communication between the people and shuttles consists of mailboxes. This communication includes hopping on(tells the person to get on and gives the person the id of the shuttle), getting off now(tells the person to get off and tells the person where they are), and got off(tells the driver whether or not they got off). The facilities and mailboxes were created dynamically, so I needed to change the syntax to handle the it.
